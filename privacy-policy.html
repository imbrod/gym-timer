Privacy Policy — Dabac Gym Timer

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Dabac Gym Timer ("the app") is developed by Dalibor Šver. This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: the app does not collect, store on any server, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

The app collects no personal data. It does not require an account, does not ask for any personal information, and does not contain any analytics, advertising or tracking technology.

Data stored on your device

Your timer settings and saved presets (work/rest durations, rounds, sounds, round names, language, etc.) are stored only locally on your device using the app's local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Internet access

The app works fully offline and does not send your data over the internet.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "Last updated" date.
